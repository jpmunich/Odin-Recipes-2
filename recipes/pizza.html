Pizza

Description

This is a pizza, one of if not THE most versatile dishes out there. Loved by the masses, you may create virtually anything you want with this masterpiece of a dish.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Assemble toppings onto uncooked dough
  2. Add any sauces or spices onto the topped pizza
  3. Throw it in the oven and cook to perfection!
