Burger

Description

The traditional American dish. You are not a true American if you have not eaten a burger. Either way, now is your chance to become one by ordering this dish!

Ingredients

  • Angus Beef Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup
  • Brioche Bun

Steps

  1. Assemble all toppings and ingredients
  2. Start with bottom patty and place patty on top
  3. Put Lettuce on top, then tomato, then ketchip, then the top bun
  4. Enjoy!
